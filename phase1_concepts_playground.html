The model doesn't "look up" facts — it generates plausible-sounding text. It's a pattern completer, not a search engine. This is why you always verify AI output for facts, numbers, and citations.

The context window is everything the model can "see" in one call — your system prompt, the conversation history, and the user's message all share it. When it's full, older messages get dropped.
